About Book Advisor

This system recommends books to users based on two main methods:

  • Popularity-Based Recommender: This method recommends books based on their popularity, i.e., the most rated books.
  • Collaborative Filtering: This method recommends books based on users' preferences. Books similar to the ones a user has liked in the past will be recommended.
